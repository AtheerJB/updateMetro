Riyadh Metro

Enhancing Transportation in Riyadh

The Riyadh Metro is a modern public transportation system designed to enhance mobility in the city. Spanning over 176 kilometers, it features six lines and 85 stations, providing efficient and reliable service to residents and visitors.

Our goal is to reduce traffic congestion, promote sustainable transport, and improve air quality in Riyadh. With advanced technology and state-of-the-art trains, the Riyadh Metro is set to revolutionize urban transit in the capital.
